Principal Investigator

Dr. Caner Geyik

Group Leader

Dr. Caner Geyik is an Assistant Professor of Medical Biochemistry at Istinye University and the Principal Investigator of GeyikLab. He holds a PhD from the Institute of Drug Abuse, Toxicology, and Pharmaceutical Sciences at Ege University, alongside BSc and MSc degrees in Biochemistry. Expanding his multidisciplinary expertise, Dr. Geyik is also currently a PhD Candidate in Medical Biology and Genetics.

His current work focuses on transcription factors and their role in cellular regulation and drug resistance, as well as on epigenetic mechanisms involved in drug resistance and biomarker discovery. His studies integrate transcriptional analysis with molecular biology and cell culture-based experimental models to identify novel prognostic markers and therapeutic targets.
