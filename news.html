Lab News & Updates

November 20, 2025

New Paper Published in JMB

We are pleased to announce that our latest study, "Regulation of Nrf2 by tsRNAs in Glioblastoma," has been accepted for publication in the Journal of Molecular Biology.

This work identifies a novel mechanism by which stress-induced tRNA fragments sequester Nrf2 in the cytoplasm, preventing antioxidant response activation.

TUBITAK Grant Awarded

Dr. Geyik has received a new 1001 grant from TUBITAK to investigate the biogenesis mechanisms of tsRNAs. This 3-year project will allow us to perform high-depth sequencing analysis of patient samples.
