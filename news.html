February 27, 2025 Project

TUBITAK Project Accepted

We are happy to announce that our project proposal titled "Effect of CLOCK Inhibition on Temozolomide Response in Glioblastoma Cells" (Glioblastoma Hücrelerinde CLOCK İnhibisyonunun Temozolomid Yanıtına Etkisi) has been accepted for funding by TUBITAK 1002/A.

In this project, circadian clock regulation in chemotherapy will be modeled in glioblastoma cell lines.

April 15, 2022 Project

TUSEB Project Accepted

We are happy to announce that our project proposal titled "The Effect of KRAS Mutation and Nrf2 Inhibition on Irinotecan Treatment in Colorectal Cancer" (Kolorektal Kanserde KRAS Mutasyonu ve Nrf2 İnhibisyonunun İrinotekan Tedavisine Etkisi) has been accepted for funding by TUSEB A (2022-ACİL-08).

This project will allow us to investigate the combinatory effects of using NRF2 inhibitors and chemotherapeutic agents in KRAS-mutant colorectal cancers.
